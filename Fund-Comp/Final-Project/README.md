=====================================================================
                           SPACE ODYSSEY
=====================================================================

THE MISSION:
You are a solo pilot navigating through a treacherous asteroid field
at the edge of unknown space. Your ship's shields can withstand only 
three impacts before catastrophic failure. How long can you survive 
in the void of the empty space?

CONTROLS:
  W       - Fire thrusters (accelerate forward)
  A       - Rotate left
  D       - Rotate right
  Q       - Quit game

GAME OVER:
  - Press Q to quit
  - Press R to restart and try again

NOTES:
  - Your ship will drifts in space 
  - Asteroids spawn from all four borders of the screen
  - Maximum velocity is fixed
  - Requires Xming (or similar X11 server) running on Windows for the gfx graphics to display
  - Requires the same gfx/X11 dependencies used by the lab graphics exercises.

Good luck, pilot. May you find safe passage through the stars.
